UG Peer-to-Peer TX — States

P2P auto or manual mode, sending to peer (connection=0). Icon: ToPeerTX (right+up arrow).

UG Peer-to-Peer RX — States

P2P auto or manual mode, receiving from peer (connection=1). Icon: ToPeerRX (down+right arrow).

UG Peer-to-Peer Bidi — States

P2P auto or manual mode, both TX and RX (connection=2). Icon: ToPeerTX + ToPeerRX layered.

UG Capture-to-Local — States

Local loopback — capture + display on same machine, no network. Icon: FromLocal (down arrow + right turn into sink).
